## Problem Description
There is lots of fake content and untruthful information that is spreading across social media platforms and online. These fake content could play a huge role in the community by influencing their decisions and leading them to false knowledge. In order to solve this issue, we will be developing a project to detect any misleading and fake content among the social media posts and news articles to lower social damage. 

## Research Questions
We will attempt to answer a couple of questions related to handling the classification of fake news.
1. What informs the model that there is misinformation in an article?
2. Why does a model believe an article is fake? (Explainable AI)
3. How is the information classified when articles are partially correct or incorrect?
4. How can we find and label subgroups in a dataset?
